Map Schedule

Housing

If you show up and no one is around, check for a note on the door.

We have two rental houses, the slight larger Sheep House and the more petite but equally wonderful Wool House. The map has locations of both houses along with other points of interest, or see the addresses below.

If you are being provided overnight accomodation you will be staying in one of the two houses. Please see your confirmation email from Triston for which house you have been assigned to. Sleeping is first come, first serve, but please be considerate of couples. Sleeping is two queens per queen bed so get cozy.

The first arrivals will be occurring Thursday evening. From that time on there is a good chance that someone will be at either location.

Sheep House
505 W Sepulveda Rd, Palm Springs, CA 92262

Wool House
635 Bedford Dr, Palm Springs, CA 92264

Transport

We will be trying to carpool using available vehicles as much as possible, especially on build day. After that we will need to rely on Lyft and Uber.

Food

Food will be delivered to the work site during build and teardown. At other times food is an individual responsibility. Both houses have typical rental house kitchen facilities that you are free to use provided you are considerate of your housemates and keep them clean.

Shift Assignments

Shift assignments will be communicated via email and posted in each house. If possible they will also be posted here eventually.

Leaving

The Pearl and Gear Truck crews will be the last to depart on Monday. Please make sure to contribute to general cleanup before you depart. It may not be the playa, but the last people out the door shouldn't need to deal with all the trash. Specific details about what needs to be done will be available in each house.
